History of TechCon

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of local tech enthusiasts. From its humble beginnings in a university lecture hall, TechCon now attracts thousands of attendees from around the globe, featuring keynote speakers from leading tech companies and groundbreaking startups. We've seen incredible advancements unfold on our stages, from the early days of mobile computing to the latest in AI and sustainable tech.

Our Mission

Our mission at TechCon is to accelerate technological progress by connecting brilliant minds, fostering collaborative innovation, and empowering the next generation of tech leaders.

Past Speakers

TechCon has been honored to host some of the most influential figures in the tech industry. Here are a few notable past speakers and their contributions:

Dr. Sharma

Dr. Sharma is a renowned AI ethicist and pioneer in responsible AI development. At TechCon 2022, she delivered a powerful keynote on the societal impact of artificial intelligence and the importance of ethical guidelines in its deployment.

Mr. David Adja

Mr. David Adja, CEO of Quantum Innovations, shared his insights on the future of quantum computing at TechCon 2023.

Ms. Ms Petra

Ms Petra, a leading expert in cybersecurity, captivated the audience at TechCon 2021 with her talk on defending against advanced persistent threats.
